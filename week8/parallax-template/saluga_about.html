About the author (that's me).
Nina Saluga is a senior studying Multiplatform Journalism and Digital Media Arts at Duquesne University. She is a freelance writer for the Herald-Standard, a daily newspaper in Uniontown, Pennsylvania that reaches beyond 168,000 readers. Off campus, she has interned at the Docherty Agency, a marketing and advertising agency based in Pittsburgh. Nina's work is primarily based in content creation, digital marketing and web/graphic design. Her ultimate goal is to professionally create and curate digital content for brands and creative agencies. To achieve it, she is currently the Public Relations intern at WorkBook Software.
